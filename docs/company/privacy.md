# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 30, 2025**

Welcome to Steedos. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how Steedos (referred to as "we," "us," or "the Platform") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you access our services via this login page.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless low-code development experience, we collect the following types of information:

* **Account Information:** Your name, email address, phone number, and encrypted credentials.
* **Organization Data:** Information regarding the tenant (Organization) you belong to and your associated roles and permissions.
* **Developer Metadata:** Configurations, object definitions, page layouts, and logic scripts (APIs/Triggers) created within the platform.
* **Usage & Security Logs:** IP addresses, browser types, timestamps, and audit logs of system activities to ensure account security and platform stability.

## 2. Data Ownership and Isolation

* **Tenant Isolation:** Steedos utilizes a robust multi-tenant architecture. Your data is logically isolated from other organizations to ensure maximum privacy.
* **Your Ownership:** You (or your organization) retain full ownership of all business data, application logic, and code assets generated on the platform. Steedos acts solely as a service provider.
* **Deployment Flexibility:** * **Steedos Cloud:** Data is stored in high-security, encrypted cloud environments.
* **Private Cloud / On-Premise:** If you are using a self-hosted version, all data remains entirely within your own infrastructure and under your direct control.



## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to:

* Provide, maintain, and improve platform features.
* Authenticate your identity and manage access control.
* Monitor system performance and prevent fraudulent or malicious activities.
* Provide technical support and respond to your inquiries.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell your personal or business data to third parties.** We only share information when:

* **Explicit Consent:** You authorize integrations with third-party services (e.g., Slack, DingTalk, or GitHub).
* **Legal Compliance:** Required by law, regulation, or legal process.
* **Service Providers:** Sub-processors (such as cloud infrastructure providers) who assist in delivering our services under strict confidentiality agreements.

## 5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols, including:

* **Encryption:** Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using SSL/TLS.
* **Access Control:** Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to ensure only authorized personnel can access specific data.
* **Regular Audits:** Frequent security patching and vulnerability assessments.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

* Access and export your configuration and business data.
* Correct or update your personal information.
* Request the deletion of your account and associated data (subject to organizational policies).
